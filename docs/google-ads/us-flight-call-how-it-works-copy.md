# How it works — LP copy for A/B test

Use this block on the landing page or in a test variant. Keep a clear **not the official airline** disclaimer nearby on the page.

## Version A — text

When you tap the number or call button, you reach our booking line and a live agent. A short qualification IVR may ask a few questions first so we can route your call to the right help. If your trip is within scope, the agent helps you search U.S. flight options, explains the total price and any applicable fees before you pay, and then completes the booking steps with you. If your request is outside scope, the call may be redirected or ended after qualification.

## Version B — bullet point

- Tap the number or call button to reach the booking line.
- A brief qualification IVR may ask a few questions first.
- A live agent helps you search U.S. flight options and compare choices.
- The total price and any applicable fees are explained before payment.
- If the request is outside scope, the call may be redirected or ended after qualification.
